Privacy Policy

App: SpeedMath Coach

Company: Caivra Tech LLC

Effective date: June 6, 2026

Overview

SpeedMath Coach is a mental math practice application designed to help users complete quick arithmetic exercises. This version of the app is designed to work locally on your device.

Information We Collect

SpeedMath Coach does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or other personal information.

This version of SpeedMath Coach does not collect personal information from users and does not send personal information to Caivra Tech LLC servers.

Local App Activity

The app may store basic app activity or preferences locally on your device, such as exercise progress, settings, or interface preferences, if those features are available in your installed version. Local data remains on your device unless you choose to delete the app or clear its data through your device settings.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

This version of SpeedMath Coach does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not use third-party analytics SDKs, and does not track users across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information from SpeedMath Coach users. Because this version does not collect personal information, there is no personal information to share with advertisers or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

SpeedMath Coach is a general-audience educational app. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

Security

We aim to keep the app simple and privacy-conscious. Since this version is designed for local use and does not require user accounts or cloud syncing, the privacy risk is reduced. No method of software operation or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as SpeedMath Coach evolves. If future versions add accounts, cloud features, analytics, advertising, purchases, or other services, this policy will be updated to explain the relevant data practices.
